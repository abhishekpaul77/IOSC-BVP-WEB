About-Us

IOSC-BVP - the first ever Intel collaborated club by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering of Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, New Delhi which aims to enhance student's skills in Technical and Non-Technical fields.

IOSC-BVP was started with the AIM of future growth in AI, ML, data-centric applications, and various domains of IOT. The industry needs a programming model that allows developers to take advantage of rapid Innovation & Creation, and IOSC is here to bridge that gap between what the Industry needs and Student's Learnings.

IOSC, what you wished existed.
  • Joining a club or a society will enable you to connect to a peer group who shares similar interests as you.

  • Being a part of a club or a society helps you to gain knowledge, skills and experience in leadership.

  • You will undoubtedly learn new skills. In a diverse group, you will be able to experience personal growth.
